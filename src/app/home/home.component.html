About Us

Craft & Harvest started as a small hobby shop making things like candles, lip balms, hand salves, and more. But as time went on, there became an apparent need of having more local items available to hobbyists and craftspeople that the Denver market simply didn’t have. Soy wax for example, an all natural, USA grown and made product, normally had to be shipped several times to several places before ever reaching candle makers in Colorado. It's expensive and inefficient but now we can say that that process has been vastly simplified. We get our product shipped directly from the facility that processes the soybeans into the soy wax and distribute in the Denver and surrounding area offering free local pickup and flat fee local deliveries. This dramatically reduces our carbon footprint and supports sustainable USA soybean farmers at the same time!

Our Goal

Our goal as a company is to provide a variety of crafting items for candle makers and crafts people alike. We are working hard to source the best, most sustainable, USA made items for all your crafting needs!
